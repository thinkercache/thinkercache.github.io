Anwesha Mohanty

I am a final year PhD Candidate at Dublin City University (DCU) as a part of CRT d-real programme funded by Science Foundation Ireland. I am advised by Dr. Alistair Sutherland, Dr. Marija Bezbradica and Dr. Hossein Javidnia. My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning with a focus on Generative Modelling, Deep Learning, and the Classification Models applying on Medical/Clinical Images.
